Dana White's Contender Series, Season 6

Episode eight (9/13/22) Brasil knocked out Marnic Mann at 4:33 of the second round

When and why did you start training for fighting? I always liked fighting. So when I was working and studying, I looked for a sport activity. I started training and never stopped.

What professional titles have you held? Brazilian kickboxing champion. 

Any accomplishments in amateur combat sports? State, South American and world kickboxing champion. Brazilian champion at purple belt.

Any belts/ ranks in grappling disciplines? BJJ brown belt

Any belts/ ranks in striking disciplines? Kickboxing black belt

What has been your toughest fight to date and why? My last fight against Loma was the most difficult. I couldn’t apply good blows due to the movement of my opponent.

Who is your favorite fighter and why? Valentina Shevchenko, for her skills and her mental strength.

Did you go to college and if so what degree did you earn? Yes. Graduated in Accounting Sciences.

What was your job before you started fighting? Administrative assistant.

Personal Life / Background: Video games, family, dog and movies.
