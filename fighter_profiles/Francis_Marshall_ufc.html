Episode two (8/2/22) Marshall won a three round unanimous decision over Connor Matthews 

When and why did you start training for fighting? I started training for fighting around 14-15 because I wanted to fight for a living and fight in the UFC

Training: Morning - fasted cardio, Afternoon - strength and conditioning, Night - MMA practice

What does it mean for you to fight in the UFC? It means everything to me. That’s what I’ve been training to get to for the last eight, nine years of my life.

What was your job before you started fighting? I never had a job before fighting

Specific accomplishments in amateur competition? Bayou FC 155-pound title

Ranks in any martial arts styles: Purple belt in BJJ

Favorite grappling technique: Rear naked choke

Favorite Striking technique: Jab
