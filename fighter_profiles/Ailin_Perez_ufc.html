UFC 311 (01/18/25) Perez won a three round unanimous decision over Karol Rosa

UFC Fight Night (9/28/24) Perez submitted Daria Zhelezniakova via arm-triangle choke at 3:52 of the first round

UFC 302 (6/1/24) Perez won a three round unanimous decision over Joselyne Edwards 

UFC Fight Night (11/18/23) Perez won a three round unanimous decision over Lucie Pudilova 

UFC on ESPN (7/15/23) Perez won a three round unanimous decision over Ashlee Evans-Smith 

UFC Fight Night (9/3/22) Perez was submitted by Stephanie Egger via rear naked choke at 4:54 of the second round 

When and why did you start training for fighting? I was 12 when I started combat sports. I started doing Kung Fu and Sanda. The reason I started martial arts was because I had multiple brothers who played Rugby, so I wanted to do a contact sport like them.

Training: I train two or three times a day with different gyms/trainers for different disciplines. This is my schedule every day from Monday to Saturday.

What ranks and titles have you held? 2-time South American MMA Bantamweight Champion, Champion in Samurai Fight House, 2-time South American Sanda Champion in FAWK at 65kg and 70kg

Do you have any heroes? My dad because he always took care of me when I needed him. He believed in me before anyone else ever did.

What does it mean for you to fight in the UFC? It is a dream come true. It proves to me that I can do anything.

What was your job before you started fighting? Fitness Instructor

Specific accomplishments in amateur competition? 1-0 as an amateur in MMA

Ranks in any martial arts styles: Purple belt in BJJ, red belt in Kung Fu

Favorite grappling technique: Ground-and-Pound

Favorite Striking technique: Pressure
